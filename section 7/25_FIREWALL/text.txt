Main simple + practical way me samjhata hoon 👇

🔥 Firewall kya hota hai?

👉 Firewall ek security guard hai jo network traffic ko control karta hai.

💡 Simple:

Kaun andar aa sakta hai aur kaun nahi → firewall decide karta hai.

🧠 Real-life analogy

🏢 Building gate par guard:

✔ allowed log → andar
❌ unknown log → entry band

Firewall bhi yehi karta hai.

🌐 Firewall kya protect karta hai?

✔ hackers
✔ unauthorized access
✔ malware traffic
✔ suspicious connections
✔ unwanted ports access

📦 Firewall ka kaam kaise hota hai?

Firewall check karta hai:

✔ IP address
✔ port number
✔ protocol (TCP/UDP)
✔ incoming/outgoing traffic

Phir decide karta hai:

✅ allow
❌ block
